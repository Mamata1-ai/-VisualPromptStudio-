# Prompt B – Humanism and the Move Toward Science

Historical Theme: Humanism and the move toward science  
Historical Period: Early Renaissance  
Artwork Inspiration: Masaccio, Trinity  
Medium: Fresco painting  

Create an Early Renaissance fresco painting showing a religious scene placed within a realistic architectural setting. The composition should use linear perspective with a clear vanishing point to create believable depth. Human figures should appear natural, proportional, and solid, showing careful observation of the body and realistic spatial relationships.

Use balanced composition, clear perspective lines, soft modeling of form, and controlled color. Include shading that gives the figures volume and presence. The architectural space should feel logical and mathematically organized, reflecting a new interest in science and realism.

The purpose of the artwork is to reflect the Early Renaissance shift toward humanism, observation, and scientific thinking while still presenting a religious subject. The painting should show how artists used perspective, anatomy, and realism to communicate truth, order, and a more human-centered understanding of the world.
